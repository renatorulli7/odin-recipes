Ingredients

  • 5 Fresh tomatoes
  • 1 Barilla Lasagna pasta
  • 150 grams of Parmesan chesse
  • 300 grams of Mozarella cheese

Steps

  1. Add a layer of Lasagna pasta
  2. Add a layer of pomodoro on top
  3. Add a layer of Mozarella cheese
  4. Repeat the steps until you run out of Lasagna pasta
  5. Add and spread the Parmesan over the Lasagna
  6. Put in a pre-heated 180 degrees oven for 40 minutes
